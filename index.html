/ About

Practical trading education built around process.

The Trade Diaries is a practical trading education platform designed to help traders build discipline, structured decision-making, and consistency through real market learning.

Our philosophy is simple: Process Over Prediction.

We believe successful trading is not built through shortcuts, random tips, or complicated indicators. It is built through process, risk management, trade review, and the ability to execute with discipline.

Built around The 7 Trade Challenge, our programs combine practical learning, guided trade review, and structured journaling to help traders improve execution quality and long-term decision-making.

Our goal is not just to teach trading — but to help build disciplined traders focused on consistency and continuous improvement.

/ Course Overview

The 7 Trade Challenge

What is The 7 Trade Challenge?

A structured trading program focused on discipline, execution, and consistency through guided learning, trade review workshops, and practical trade journaling.

The goal is not to take more trades — but to take better trades.

01 Foundation

Market Basics

Stock market basics, commodity concepts, chart reading, candlesticks, and market structure.

02 Execution

Structured Trade Execution

Learn how to identify quality setups, plan entries, define stop loss and targets, and execute trades using a disciplined process.

03 Review

Trade Review & Journal

Review trades through a structured Trade Diary focused on execution quality, mistakes, emotional awareness, and continuous improvement.

Duration8 Weeks, every Sunday
FormatFlexible Batches - Online & Offline

Mission
